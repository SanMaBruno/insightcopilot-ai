# E-Commerce Product Strategy — Q1-Q2 2024

## Overview

This document summarizes the strategic direction for our product catalog in Q1-Q2 2024. It focuses on category performance, pricing strategy, and regional expansion plans.

## Category Strategy

### Electronics

Electronics is our highest-revenue category, driven by flagship items like the Laptop Pro 15 and Monitor 4K 27". Key priorities:

- Maintain competitive pricing on high-margin items (laptops, monitors, headphones).
- Expand the mid-range segment ($50-$150) with accessories like USB-C hubs and wireless chargers.
- Phase out low-performing SKUs with ratings below 3.5.

### Furniture

Furniture shows strong average order value but lower volume. Strategy:

- Focus on ergonomic products (chairs, standing desks, monitor arms) as remote work demand continues.
- Bundle furniture items with electronics for cross-selling opportunities.
- Target corporate buyers with bulk pricing on office furniture.

### Accessories

Accessories drive high volume but low individual revenue. Approach:

- Use accessories as entry-point products to acquire new customers.
- Create accessory bundles to increase average order value.
- Optimize shipping costs since margins are thin.

## Pricing Guidelines

| Category     | Target Margin | Price Range     |
|-------------|---------------|-----------------|
| Electronics | 25-35%        | $25 - $1,300    |
| Furniture   | 30-40%        | $23 - $700      |
| Accessories | 40-50%        | $10 - $40       |

Products with ratings above 4.5 can sustain a 5-10% premium. Products below 3.5 should be reviewed for discontinuation or improvement.

## Regional Focus

### North America
- Primary market. Focus on premium electronics and furniture.
- Leverage fast shipping as competitive advantage.

### Europe
- Growing market. Expand mid-range electronics.
- Assess VAT-inclusive pricing for better conversion.

### Asia
- Emerging market. Lead with accessories and mid-range electronics.
- Partner with local distributors for last-mile delivery.

## Inventory Management

- Products marked as out_of_stock should be restocked within 2 weeks or removed.
- Items with zero or null units_sold need review — either marketing push or discontinuation.
- Maintain minimum 30 days of stock for top-selling items (units_sold > 500).

## Key Metrics to Watch

1. Revenue per category per region
2. Average rating trend (target: maintain above 4.0 overall)
3. Stock-out frequency
4. Customer acquisition cost by product entry point
5. Cross-sell conversion rate on bundles

## Next Steps

- Prepare Q3 catalog adjustments based on Q1-Q2 learnings.
- Evaluate partnership opportunities with 2-3 regional distributors in Asia.
